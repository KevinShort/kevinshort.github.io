Hello. Thank you for visiting my corner of the web.

My name is Christine. I am the mother of two mostly grown boys and wife to my high school sweetheart. I was inspired to start crocheting by watching my grandmother crochet while I was a child. Although I had dabbled in crocheting I had not done it in a while due to my children’s active lifestyles.

I was diagnosed with breast cancer early in 2012. Sitting on the couch and watching TV when I was not feeling well just wasn’t cutting it for me so I decided to start crocheting again.

Well, once I started I just couldn’t stop, so here I am, selling my wares on the internet. Please make yourself comfortable and take a look around. Thank you for visiting Chris’ Crochet Corner.
